The Basics

Welcome to this collage creator application thing (Name pending)! First things first, let me explain the basics.

With this application you can create large images made up of lots of little images, like a collage or mosaic.

The clever bit is, you can choose what these images are!

 

Fancy making a family photo out of your holiday pictures? Go right ahead!

Want to make your company logo out of picture of cheese? That’s fine by me.

And yes Tom Maher, you can make a big picture of Ainsley Harriott out of little pictures of Ainsley Harriott if you must.

 

This is of course assuming you have the pictures of these things at your disposal (saved on your computer).

If you don’t, no problem! Just download them from the internet first or something.

Using the program

First make sure you have an idea of what picture you want to make. This will be your template image. Click “Select New Template” to import this image.

Next you need to import the images that you want to make your template out of. Do this by clicking “Select New Images”. You can select multiple at a time.

Now that you have your images imported, you can edit what you’ve selected. Simply click on an image to select or deselect it. Selected images are highlighted in blue, deselected in red. Deselected images will not be included in the output generation.

When you’re ready, press “Generate”. This will begin the collage generation process. A window will pop up giving you generation preferences. They are each explained in the application.

When you’ve done this press “Begin Generation”. At this point, you might be in for a long wait depending on your generation preferences, so go get a cup of tea.

Eventually the generation will be complete and you will see your output image in a new window. Here you will be given options of what to do next. I recommend immediately saving it before doing anything else!
